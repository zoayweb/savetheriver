  • CLIMATE LITERACY FOR SCHOOLS, COLLEGES, AND UNIVERSITIES

Save The Rivers Movement, Bangladesh is passionate about building a climate literate future generation. In early July of 2019, a landmark ruling of The Supreme Court of Bangladesh made her the first country to GRANT ALL ITS RIVERS the same legal status as humans. From now on, its rivers will be treated as living entities in a court of law.

The landmark ruling by the Bangladeshi Supreme Court also includes a critical directive that requires all the educational institutes of Bangladesh to educate students about rivers.

Save The Rivers Movement, Bangladesh has a robust curriculum to develop climate literacy among the school, college, and university students.
